Who We Are

Are you in need of service from artisans for a quick fix or are you an artisan who wants to offer service to users ? Then you're welcome to Hirepesin.


Hirepesin is an innovative platform that is focused on connecting artisans and local craftsman from different parts of Africa to recruiters from various users who are in need of services or quick fix jobs. We want to break the chain of unemployment and under employment amongst the youths and artisans.


Hirepesin was founded by Jeff Dauda and Ahmed Bankole with the intention of providing an interactive platform for both artisans and users looking for services. Our goal is to provide professional services to users.

How It Works

Search For Artisans.

Using Geolocation data from Google Maps, we can help to pin-point and trace professional handymen or artisans near your area or locality and save you the stress of looking for one.

Get Proposals.

After posting or creating an offer on Hirepesin for the service you are in need of. You will receive an email address regarding the proposals from the artisans who applied to your job offer.

Accept Proposals.

You can choose to accept or decline proposals from artisans if they don't meet certian criteria like experience or price. You can also bargain prices or message artisans.

Get Your Work Done.

Once you have successfully accepted the proposal from the artisan then you can progress and get your work done within a short period of time. No stress, No yawa and No struggle.
